Kennet Facts

  • Established 1997. Offices in London, Silicon Valley, Frankfurt
  • Approximately $700 million under management
  • Invest in fast-growing technology, services and digital media businesses across Europe and North America
  • Strong track record of building market-leading companies. See Portfolio.

Investment Principles

  • Belief in the founder-entrepreneur
  • Respect for capital-efficient businesses
  • Recognition that technology markets are global
  • A focus on sales productivity as the engine of growth
  • Flexibility in finding the right structure for each deal

Kennet Culture

  • Belief in the power of coaching to develop world-class, founder-led businesses
  • Openness to new ideas
  • Attitude of transparency, healthy scepticism and debate
  • Conviction that top-quality companies can be built from anywhere
